About Us

Starworld Commodities Pte Ltd, is a company formed by by a team of veteran Senior Commodity Traders, with combined experience of more than 120 years. We are based out of Singapore. It is a cash rich company, with reach for Metals and Paper in more than 60 countries. We are backed by some of the biggest Mills in Asia, to serve their bulk requirements in containerised steel scrap, Non-Ferrous SCrap scrap and paper scrap.

With the help of our experienced and thorough logistics team, we engage directly with the biggest shipping lines and do our own trucking and customs in most of the procurement countries. We have our own Inspectors for loadings at most origin sites.

Starworld Commodity Pte. Ltd. (SWC) incorporated in Jan 2021 is a premier global commodity trading house bearing a wide network across various continents. Under the stewardship of Management Team having an experience of more than a decade in international commodity trading, SWC has been trading in diversified portfolios dealing in products such as Steel & Steel Scrap, paper scrap, aluminium scrap.

The key focus of our experienced and efficient professionals is to create long term and sustainable values for our suppliers, customers, employees and all business partners.
